ABU Robocon 2025: A New Frontier in Robot Basketball

The ABU Robocon 2025 is here, and it’s bigger, better, and more exciting than ever! This year’s theme, "Robot Basketball: Team Effort, Ultimate Success," highlights the importance of collaboration, strategy, and innovation in the world of robotics. Held in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia, the competition brings together the brightest engineering minds from across Asia-Pacific to showcase their ingenuity and teamwork.

Game Overview: Robot Basketball

Inspired by traditional basketball, the ABU Robocon 2025 introduces a high-tech twist to the sport. Teams of robots compete to score points by shooting basketballs into their opponent's basket while defending their own. Here are the key highlights:

  • Team Composition: Two robots, three students, one instructor, and up to three pit crew members.
  • Gameplay Duration: 120 seconds for preliminaries, 160 seconds for knockouts.
  • Scoring System:
    • 3 points: Shots from the 3-point zone.
    • 2 points: Shots closer to the basket.
    • 7 points: Spectacular dunk shots.

Key Rules and Specifications

The ABU Robocon 2025 Rulebook lays down detailed guidelines for fair play and technical standards. Here’s an overview:

Robot Specifications

  • Dimensions: Robots must fit within an 800 mm diameter cylinder and a height of 1500 mm before the game. During play, they can extend to 1200 mm in diameter and 2400 mm in height.
  • Weight: Combined weight of both robots must not exceed 50 kg.
  • Power Sources: Only batteries, compressed air, or elastic force are allowed, with a maximum voltage of 24V.

Game Field

  • Dimensions: 15m x 8m playing area with specific zones for operating and spare mechanisms.
  • Equipment: Regulation basketballs, backboards, and visible shot clocks.

Safety First

Safety is a top priority. Here’s how teams ensure a safe competition:

  • Robots must include an emergency “STOP” button.
  • Participants must wear helmets, safety goggles, and running shoes.
  • Prohibited items include lead-acid batteries, explosives, and unsafe lasers.

Event Schedule

The competition spans multiple days to cover testing, rehearsals, and the main event:

  • August 22, 2025: Arrival and registration.
  • August 23, 2025: Test runs and rehearsals.
  • August 24, 2025: Contest Day.
  • August 25, 2025: Meetings, workshops, and exchange programs.
  • August 26, 2025: Departure.

Why ABU Robocon 2025 Matters

ABU Robocon is more than a competition; it’s a platform for creativity, teamwork, and technical excellence. Participants gain invaluable experience in robotics, problem-solving, and international collaboration. Don’t miss the opportunity to witness cutting-edge innovation in action!
